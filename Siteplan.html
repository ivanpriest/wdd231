Website Site Plan

Bayside Book Haven - Your Literary Sanctuary

1. Site Name

Bayside Book Haven

Reason for selection: This name was chosen because it evokes a sense of peace and community ("Haven"), location relevance ("Bayside"), and clearly indicates the site's focus on books and literature. It suggests a welcoming space for book lovers to gather, discover, and share their passion for reading.

Optional domain availability: baysidebookhaven.com

2. Site Purpose

The Bayside Book Haven website serves as a digital community center for local book enthusiasts. It will provide:

  • Weekly book recommendations and reviews from community members
  • Calendar of local book club meetings and literary events
  • Platform for members to share their own book reviews and ratings
  • Directory of local independent bookstores and libraries
  • Online forum for book discussions and literary conversations
  • Monthly featured author highlights and interviews

3. Scenarios

These questions represent what target audience members might ask when visiting the site:

Scenario 1: New Resident

"I just moved to the area and love reading. Where can I find local book clubs to join and meet fellow readers?"

This visitor needs clear navigation to the events calendar and book club directory, with filtering options by genre, meeting frequency, and location.

Scenario 2: Avid Reader

"I just finished a great historical fiction novel and want to find similar recommendations. Can I also share my review with others?"

This user needs access to the recommendation engine, search by genre features, and an easy way to submit their own reviews to the community.

Scenario 3: Local Bookstore Owner

"How can I promote my store's upcoming author signing event to the local reading community?"

This user needs clear information about submitting events, contact forms for business owners, and visibility of similar successful events.

4. Color Schema

The following color palette creates a calm, readable environment suitable for a literary website:

Primary
#F7FFF7

Usage: Main background color throughout the site. Provides clean, easy reading experience.

Secondary
#5F5F58

Usage: Headers, footers, navigation bars, and section dividers. Also used for some buttons and accents.

Tertiary
#343434

Usage: Primary text color, headings, and important interactive elements.

This document exclusively uses the selected color scheme as demonstrated above.

5. Typography

Three fonts have been selected to create hierarchy and visual interest:

Noto Serif

Usage: All headings (H1, H2, H3, H4), section titles, and important labels.

Example: "Welcome to Bayside Book Haven"

Barlow

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, lists, navigation items, and form inputs.

Example: This is how regular paragraph text will appear throughout the website for optimal readability.

Dancing Script

Usage: Decorative elements, quotes, special highlights, and the site tagline.

Example: "Where every book finds its reader"

This document demonstrates the actual use of these fonts as specified above.
